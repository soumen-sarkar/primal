OUR PRIORITY IS TO
SEE YOU IN FINE FORM
AGAIN.

Primal function depends on the most primitive form of movement. When there is injury, pain or imbalance, movement is affected. Our priority is to see you in fine form again.


We’ll set a game plan for you to work through and connect you with the right treatments to relieve pain, improve function, mobility and strength. It’s a collaborative process with our team of practitioners and specialists, who use an integrated approach to assess what isn’t working properly and work with you to achieve optimal results.

Our goal is not just to get you out of pain, but also to take you through the process of being broken, to healing, to preventing further damage in the future.
